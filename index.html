FAQ

Common questions.

How long does a typical project take?
Most projects ship in 2–6 weeks. Landing sites and CMS builds are usually 1–2 weeks; custom Laravel + Vue apps and MIS systems run 4–8 weeks. I commit to a fixed timeline before any code is written.
How do you handle payments?
50% deposit to start, 50% on launch. Projects longer than 8 weeks are split into 3–4 milestone payments. I accept GCash, PayPal, Wise, and direct bank transfer (PH or international).
Do you work with clients outside the Philippines?
Yes — about a third of my projects are international. I’m in GMT+8 with comfortable overlap into Australia, Singapore, EU mornings, and US evenings. Async communication is the default.
Who owns the code after delivery?
You do. Full source code, full IP, no licensing fees, no vendor lock-in. On launch day I hand over the repository, deployment credentials, and a written handover doc.
What if I need changes after launch?
30 days of free fixes are baked into every project. After that, you can hire me ad-hoc per task or take a small monthly retainer — no pressure either way.
Can you take over a project someone else built?
Yes — takeover work is a normal part of what I do. I start with a short paid code audit so I can quote properly and flag any landmines before we commit to scope.
