Process

How we'll work together.

  1. 01

    Chat

    Day 0 · Free

    Free 15-minute call or WhatsApp. You describe the problem; I ask the questions that matter. No pressure, no pitch.

  2. 02

    Plan & quote

    Within 24 hours

    I send a written scope, timeline, and fixed price. You'll know exactly what's included before any work starts — no surprise charges later.

  3. 03

    Build

    2 – 6 weeks typical

    I work in milestones, usually 2–4. You see progress every few days on a staging link — not at the end. Async updates by WhatsApp or email.

  4. 04

    Review & refine

    Iterate together

    You test on staging. We iterate until it's right. Revisions inside the agreed scope are part of the deal — not extras.

  5. 05

    Launch & support

    30 days free fixes

    I deploy to your hosting, hand over credentials and docs, and stay available for 30 days of free fixes. Optional retainer after that.
